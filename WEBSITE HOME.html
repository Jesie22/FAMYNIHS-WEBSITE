About Us

Famy National Integrated High School is a public high school located in the municipality of Famy, Laguna in the Philippines. The school is committed to providing quality education to its students and has a strong focus on academic excellence and social responsibility. Famy National Integrated High School offers various academic tracks, including Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM), and Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), among others. The school is also proud of its strong sports and cultural programs, which promote a well-rounded education and foster the holistic development of its students.

FNIHS BACKGROUND

Famy National Integrated High School (FNIHS) is a public secondary school located in the municipality of Famy, Laguna, Philippines. It was established in 1986 and offers a range of academic programs from junior high school to senior high school, including technical-vocational courses. The school aims to provide quality education and produce competent and responsible graduates who can contribute to nation-building. FNIHS has a diverse student population and is known for its active involvement in community service projects and extracurricular activities.

FNIHS has several rooms and facilities to support the education and development of its students. The school has spacious classrooms that can accommodate up to 50 students per room. Each classroom is equipped with a whiteboard and a projector to aid in teaching and learning. FNIHS also has science laboratories, computer laboratories, and a library. The science laboratories are well-equipped with laboratory apparatus, instruments, and chemicals for conducting experiments and investigations in Chemistry, Physics, and Biology. The computer laboratories are equipped with the latest technology and software programs to support the teaching of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) subjects. The library has a vast collection of books, reference materials, and multimedia resources to support the research and academic needs of the students.

FNIHS HISTORY

Famy National Integrated High School, originally a small farm, transformed into a place of knowledge and wisdom. Under President Ferdinand Marcos' regime, Presidential Decree no. 479, s. 1974, was signed into law in 1962. In 1969, the principal of Famy Elementary School, Mr. Nemecio Abary, and other educators, along with district supervisors and parents, opened a new school with a small population of 64 students. The school was later named Famy Community High School, and a Marcos Type Building was built for knowledge enhancement and skills development. The school's first recognition was as champion in the Citizen Army Training Competition. Mrs. Elfleda Real became principal in 1978, and Mrs. Ligaya Manaig served as Officer-in-Charge in 1983. From 1985 to 2013, Mrs. Lourdes served as the school's mother, raising the standards of education. The school was renamed Famy National High School in 1994, but was unable to celebrate its Silver Founding Anniversary due to Typhoon Katrina.

MISSION AND VISION

THE DEPED MISSION

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where:

  • Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment.
  • Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.
  • Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.
  • Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.

THE DEPED VISION

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.
