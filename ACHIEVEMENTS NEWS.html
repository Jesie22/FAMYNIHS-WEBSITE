FUN IN THE CANTEEN: 'SHOOT THE BOTTLE' - WHERE SUSTAINABILITY MEETS ENTERTAINMENT!

In a world drowning in plastic waste, an audacious solution emerges: shooting plastic bottles into oblivion with eco-friendly ammo. It’s like a quirky action movie, but for the environment. However, remember, this isn’t a Wild West shootout; strict rules and safety gear are essential. But here’s the serious twist: We need your help to refine and responsibly implement these innovative ideas. Students, activists, and eco-enthusiasts, let’s join forces, innovate, and fight plastic pollution. Our future depends on it. #FNIHS_SSLG

CELEBRATING WORK ETHICS:A UNIFYING BANQUET

The SHS Banquet Night, organized by the SHS Society, was a memorable event filled with fun, engagement, and socialization. This formal gathering not only celebrated the achievements of Senior High School students for the School Year 2023-2024 but also recognized outstanding performances in research writing, work immersion, journalism, and various competitions. The night was highlighted by the crowning of the first SHS Banquet awardees in categories like Face of the Night and Best in Runway. Additionally, students were inspired by a young professional's talk on thriving in the 21st-century workforce, all hosted by one of Famy, Laguna's most talented emcees.

PUNO NG BUHAY PARA SA KALIKASAN

Famy NIHS – SGC embarks on its first tree planting activity Famy NIHS - School Governing Council conducted a Tree Planting Activity with the theme “Puno ng Buhay Para sa Kalikasan” on March 9, 2024 at Brgy. Balitoc, Famy, Laguna.This event is in partnership with Sangguniang Barangay of Balitoc, Famy Laguna headed by Brgy. Captain Geline Fulgueras, Famy SK Federation led by Hon. Earl Grenz Manuel, Famy Municipal Police Station, Famy Crime Watch Force, School Parents Teachers Association, Supreme Secondary Learner Government, Youth for Environment in School Organization, Faculty Club Association, Volunteers and School Stakeholders.This tree planting is one of the school’s programs to demonstrate and extend commitments to the environment and the community as well. FAMY NIHS GRADE 12 STUDENTS IMMERSE THEMSELVES IN WORK IMMERSION SEMINAR

Famy National Integrated High School (FNIHS) warmly held its first work immersion seminar with the theme “Charting Paths in Work Immersion: Strengthening Students’ Skills and Ethics in Navigating Work Realities” at Campus 2 Covered Court, February 27, 2024 Headed by the School Principal, Dr. Socorro R. Fundivilla; Grade 12 Head Teacher, Dr. Shierazade R. Velasco; SHS Coordinator, Mrs. Janette G. De Ramos; Work Immersion Focal Person, Mr. Marlon L. Coronia; and all the Grade 12 Department teachers joined together for the success of the program Moreover, the first speaker, Mr. Jerome J. Salay, Teacher III, from Balian Integrated National High School discussed work ethics, expected behavior in the workplace, and workplace rights and responsibilities, giving focus on having the right guidance and principles in the field to be a good worker.
